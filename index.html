University of Wisconsin-Madison Sprocket Lab Reading Group

The Sprocket Lab Reading Group is an occasional, informal meeting to discuss interesting papers. Visitors welcome!

To suggest a paper, open an issue on the Github page. We’ll close the issue when we schedule the paper.
